The four rules that matter
Everything else is noise. These are non-negotiable on keto.
🚫
Keep carbs under 25g/day
This is the threshold for ketosis. One slice of bread breaks it. Read labels.
🥑
Fat is your fuel now
Don't fear fat. Avocado, olive oil, eggs, and fatty cuts are your staples.
💧
Hydrate + electrolytes
Add salt to food. The "keto flu" is almost always dehydration and low sodium.
📅
Consistency beats perfection
One bad meal won't break you. Getting kicked out of ketosis and back in repeatedly does.
